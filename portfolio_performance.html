Data Sources: US Equity: S&P 500 Index (SPX). International Equity: iShares MSCI EAFE ETF (EFA). US Bonds: iShares Core US Aggregate Bond ETF (AGG). Daily closing prices sourced from the Wall Street Journal. Overlapping data begins September 26, 2003.

Methodology: Portfolios are rebalanced quarterly to target weights. A 1% annual advisory fee is deducted daily (1%/252 trading days). Within the equity allocation, 80% is allocated to US equity (S&P 500) and 20% to international equity (EFA). Returns shown are hypothetical back-tested results and do not represent actual client portfolios.

Important: Back-tested performance is hypothetical and has inherent limitations. It does not reflect actual trading, liquidity constraints, taxes, or all fees. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Actual client results will differ based on timing, contributions/withdrawals, tax circumstances, and specific investment vehicles used. ETF proxy returns do not include dividends for the index; actual index returns with dividends reinvested would be higher.

Indexes cannot be invested in directly. The S&P 500, MSCI EAFE, and Bloomberg US Aggregate Bond Index are unmanaged indexes. Model portfolios use ETFs as proxies for these asset classes.
